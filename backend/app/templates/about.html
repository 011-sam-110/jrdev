About JrDev

We're building the bridge between ambitious student developers and local businesses needing modern digital solutions.

rocket_launch

Our Mission

JrDev connects student developers with real business projects. Students gain invaluable hands-on experience, build their portfolios, and earn while learning. Businesses get cost-effective, innovative digital solutions built by hungry young talent.

groups

How It Works

Businesses post project ideas as "sprints" — short, focused prototyping challenges. Student developers sign up to build proof-of-concepts. At the end of the sprint, businesses review the prototypes and can choose to hire developers for continued work.

shield

Trust & Safety

Payments are held in escrow until sprint completion and approval. Both parties are protected with clear contracts, trial periods, and a support team available for mediation.
