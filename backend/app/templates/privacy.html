Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 February 2026

  • 01 Information We Collect

    When you register an account on JrDev, we collect your username, email address, and an encrypted version of your password. If you are a business user, we may also collect payment information via Stripe (card details are stored by Stripe, not by us).

    Developer profiles may include optional information such as your location, tech stack, social links, a profile picture, and portfolio content you choose to share.

  • 02 How We Use Your Information

    • •To provide and maintain the JrDev platform and your account.
    • •To facilitate sprint listings, contract generation, and developer–business matching.
    • •To process payments securely through Stripe.
    • •To send important notifications about your account, sprints, or contracts.
    • •To improve the platform based on aggregated, anonymised usage data.

  • 03 Data Sharing

    We do not sell your personal data. Information is shared only:

    • •With other users as necessary for the platform (e.g. a business sees a developer’s profile when they join a sprint).
    • •With Stripe for payment processing.
    • •If required by law or to protect our legal rights.

  • 04 Data Security

    Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. Sessions are signed with a secure secret key. Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe and never stored on our servers. Two-factor authentication is available for additional account security.

  • 05 Your Rights

    You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. You can delete your account and all associated data by emailing support.

  • 06 Cookies

    JrDev uses session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

  • 07 Changes to This Policy

    We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

  • 08 Contact

    If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please visit our Support page or email support@jrdev.io.
